Mantralayam


Mantralayam is a famous pilgrimage town located in the Kurnool district of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is renowned for the sacred Brindavana of Sri Raghavendra Swamy, a revered Hindu saint and philosopher. Thousands of devotees visit Mantralayam every year to seek blessings and experience its spiritual atmosphere.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Sri Raghavendra Swamy Mutt – The main pilgrimage center and spiritual hub.
  2. Raghavendra Swamy Brindavana – Sacred resting place of Sri Raghavendra Swamy.
  3. Panchamukhi Anjaneya Temple – Historic temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman.
  4. Tungabhadra River – A holy river where devotees take ritual baths.
  5. Bichali Village – Known for its connection with Sri Appanacharya, a disciple of Raghavendra Swamy.

Must-Try Foods

  • Pulihora – Traditional tamarind rice offered as prasadam.
  • Curd Rice – A simple and refreshing South Indian dish.
  • Dosa – Crispy rice-and-lentil crepe served with chutney and sambar.
  • Vada – Savory fried snack made from lentils.
  • Payasam – Sweet dessert prepared with milk, rice, and dry fruits.
